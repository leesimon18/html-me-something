A little bit about myself, Moxie!

A brilliant yet slightly timid Border Colie/Ausie Cattle Dog mix - Moxie (AKA: Mox, Moxie Boo, Boo Boo, etc...).
01.17.2017

Hi! My Name is Moxie! I live with my two humans, Angelique and Simon. They've been my human mommy and daddy for about 3 and 1/2 years. To get to know me a bit better, I'll start from the very beginning.

My biological father was a Border Colie and my mother a Ausie Cattle Dog.

My parents unfortunatly were both given up to th Jefferson County Humane Society, due to their humans unable to support them. Thankfully they were in the shelter together.


Where I was born!

I was born in the shelter on July 2nd, 2013 on the outskirts of Kansas City, which I still consider myself as contry folk. I come from a litter of 7 and I was the second largest, following the coat-tails of my big brother Rocket.


My first set of keys to drive off into the sunset!

My humans choose me just from pictures online. They didn't even second guess to decide on my other siblings. I guess it was love at first sight.


Practicing my yoga, downward-dog.


Bliss is in a belly rub.

I travled to my new home on Oct 12, 2013. There I would learn to potty outside, play ball, clean-up my toys, fly and catch my frisbee. Did I tell you how much I love flying through the air and catching my ball or frisbee. I also learned how to go through my food toys. And just between you and me... these food toys are way too easy. Now we're up to speed!
